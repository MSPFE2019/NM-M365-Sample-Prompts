## 📌 Overview

This repository contains example prompts designed for:

- State of New Mexico employees  
- Microsoft 365 Copilot **Licensed Users**  
- Government Community Cloud (GCC) environments  

These prompts are intended to be used directly inside:

✅ Outlook  
✅ Microsoft Word  

Prompts in this guide support common State of New Mexico workflows such as:

- Inter‑agency communication  
- Policy development  
- Procurement reviews  
- Grant coordination  
- Audit preparation  
- Project reporting  
- Stakeholder engagement  
- Internal documentation

## 📧 Outlook Prompts

### Inbox Triage
- Summarize emails related to the [project name] from the past week and identify any action items that require follow-up.

### Draft Response
- Draft a professional response acknowledging receipt and confirming our department will review the request.

### Meeting Preparation
- Help me prepare for my upcoming meeting by summarizing recent related email conversations.

### Meeting Invite Creation
- Create a meeting invite from this email thread including participants and a proposed agenda.

### Weekly Project Update
- Draft a weekly update email based on recent project-related email conversations.

### Procurement Follow‑Up
- Draft an email requesting clarification on submitted procurement documentation.

### Grant Coordination
- Create an email update summarizing progress on the [grant program name] initiative.

### Stakeholder Communication
- Draft an email requesting stakeholder feedback on the attached policy draft.

### Audit Request
- Draft an email requesting documentation needed for the upcoming audit review.

### Compliance Reminder
- Draft a reminder email for upcoming reporting requirements.

### Inter‑Agency Coordination
- Draft an email requesting a coordination meeting with the [agency name].

### Risk Communication
- Draft an email summarizing identified project risks and requesting mitigation strategies.

### Task Assignment
- Draft an email assigning responsibilities for the next phase of this project.

### Internal Approval
- Draft an email requesting approval for the attached program update.

### Budget Discussion
- Create an email summarizing recent budget discussions.

### Constituent Inquiry
- Draft a response to a constituent inquiry regarding program eligibility requirements.

### Training Announcement
- Draft an announcement email for upcoming staff training sessions.

### Leadership Update
- Draft a concise executive update email based on recent conversations.

### Vendor Communication
- Draft an email requesting additional documentation from a vendor.

### Meeting Follow‑Up
- Draft a follow-up email summarizing today’s meeting and include next steps.
